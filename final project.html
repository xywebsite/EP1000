Final Project

I will be documenting the process of my final project.
The final project needs to be made with a microcontroller, input and output devices, laser cut housing and 3d printed components.

I decided to make a simple mood lamp as I did not want to make anything too complicated due to lack of experience and time.

Acrylic Lamp Design

I went to look up designs that I liked, and decided on this flower.
The petals reminded me of a lotus flower.
I placed the image into adobe illustrator and added my own style to it after tracing it.

Coding for Neopixels

I coded the LED as such.

The default colour of the light is green.

It will change to blue on the first press, and red on the second press, and then purple on the third press.

Then it automatically cycles through green to blue to red to purple at the end on the fourth press.
